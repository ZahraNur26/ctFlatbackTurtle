CORE Values

Core values are the main aspect in KSP way.

Diversity

The understanding that every individual is unique as well as recognizing and embracing our differences. Our differences are what bring us together and allow us to achieve greatness.

Commitment

The act of committing, is voluntarily dedicating yourself to a cause. Committing yourself is the only way to get a task done, every person in a team needs to be committed in order for the group to succeed.

Consistency

Consistency is the condition of sticking to the same conditions and form. When you find something that works, you have to be consistent with it to make sure the task is carried out at a high level.

Innovation

To be thinking outside of the box at new, further development ideas. Accepting that a group needs to be innovative is the key to all group discussions and projects.

Curiosity

To be curious is to have a strong desire to learn, or understand something.
